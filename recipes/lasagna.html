Lasagna

Description

A very simple, yet delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna sheets in hot water until it is soft.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce. Cook the sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it in a pan for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with the lasagna sheets until you are finished. Then, bake until the top is golden brown!
